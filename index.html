Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximatley 10 minutes
  • Prepatation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan so they evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When edges set but center is soft, add your chosen fillings.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette and cook another minute before sliding onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional seasoning if needed.
